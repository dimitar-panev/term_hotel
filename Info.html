Info

Information when arriving, pricing and questions.

When arriving

When arriving you will be welcomed by our staff so we suggest you wait for them.
After they take your luggage is taken care of, you can head for the reception

Navigating through the hotel

When you enter the hotel, you will immediately see the reception which is located right in front of the entrance.

Cafeteria

Hange also has a cafeteria that is located on the right side of the reception while you are facing it.

About the cafeteria:

  1. Cheap prices
  2. Fresh food
  3. Friendly staff
  4. Comfortable chairs
  5. Lounge Bar

Souvenir shop

You didn't think there would be a souvenir shop did you? Well there is.
It is located behind the reception but if you can't find your way to it you can just ask someone from the reception and they will guide you.


Gym

If you would like to exercise and train while on a vacation in our hotel, you can visit our gym. The 50 square feet gym is perfect for any exercise because we got everything you need!
